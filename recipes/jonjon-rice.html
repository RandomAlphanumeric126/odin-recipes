JonJon's Favorite Rice

Description

This alternative take on steamed rice does away with the traditional method of steaming in water in favor of a combined variety of flavorful liquids that incorporate layers of complexity with a bold flavor that anyone could love. Soy souce, chicken stock, mirin, and dashi come together to create an umami-rific dish not easily forgotten. With the addition of the right seasonings, this dish goes to another next level.

Ingredients

  • Cold water (for rinsing)
  • 1 cup Basmati rice
  • 3/4 cup Chicken Stock
  • 3/4 cup Dashi Stock
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp mirin
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice conc.
  • 1 tbsp water
  • 2 tbsp broccoli buds
  • 1 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice under cool running water until water drains clear, and drain well.
  2. Add all ingredients to a covered 2qt pot and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes. Keep covered for the duration.
  4. Remove heat and fluff.
